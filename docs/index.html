InContext

Explain any selection — grounded in the whole page, not just the snippet. Multi‑turn, multilingual, with optional cited web search.

What it does

Select a sentence on any web page. Instead of translating that isolated snippet, InContext extracts the entire article and asks your chosen LLM to explain what the passage means in this specific article — the author's intent, implied meaning, and what each term refers to here. Then keep asking follow‑up questions, in any language.

FAQ

How is this different from a selection translator?

Translators send only the snippet. InContext uses the whole article as context to explain what the passage means here.

Is my data private?

Yes — bring‑your‑own‑key. Keys and conversations stay in your browser; page text goes only to the endpoint you configure. No backend of ours.

Which models are supported?

Any OpenAI‑compatible endpoint plus native Anthropic Claude — 15+ presets.

Can it search the web?

Optionally — a search engine fetches data, your model writes the cited answer.
